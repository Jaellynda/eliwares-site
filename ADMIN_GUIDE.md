## Admin Panel Features

The admin dashboard has three main tabs:

### 1. Product Management

**Adding Products:**
1. Click **"Add Product"** button
2. Fill in the following details:
   - **Product Name**: The name of your bathroom item
   - **Category**: Choose from Tiles, Toilets, Sinks, Taps, Showers, or Fixtures
   - **Brand**: Enter the brand name (e.g., Grohe, Duravit, Himark)
   - **Description**: Detailed product specifications
   - **Price**: Product cost in UGX
   - **Unit**: "Each", "Per SQM", or "Per Meter"
   - **Status**: In Stock, Out of Stock, or Coming Soon
   - **Image URL** (optional): Link to product image

3. Click **"Add Product"** to save

**Deleting Products:**
- Each product card has a **"Delete"** button to remove it from inventory

---

### 2. Brand Management

**Adding Luxury & Regular Brands:**

Your site already includes:
- **Luxury Brands**: Grohe, Duravit, Himark, LuxBath
- **Mid-Range**: CeramicPro, AquaLux  
- **Value Brands**: SteelMaster, FlowTech, FixPro

**To Add New Brands:**
1. Click the **"Brands"** tab
2. Click **"Add Brand"**
3. Enter:
   - **Brand Name**: Company name
   - **Country**: Country of origin
   - **Description**: What products they specialize in
   - **Tier**: Luxury, Mid-Range, or Value/Budget
4. Click **"Add Brand"** to save

---

### 3. Image Management

**Uploading Product Images:**
1. Click the **"Image Management"** tab
2. Click the upload area or drag & drop images
3. You can upload multiple images at once
4. Images are stored and can be referenced when creating products
5. Click **"Remove"** to delete unwanted images
